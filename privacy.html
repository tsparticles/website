4. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

4.1 Right to Know

You have the right to know what personal data we hold about you and how we use it.

4.2 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

4.3 Right to Rectification

You can request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.

4.4 Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to certain exceptions.

4.5 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data.

4.6 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a portable format to transfer to another service.

4.7 Right to Object

You can object to certain types of data processing, including marketing.

4.8 Right to Withdraw Consent

You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking on our cookie banner or contacting us. This won't affect data already processed.
